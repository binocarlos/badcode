# The Wank Tank

Tarquin's car — a blacked-out BMW X8, the oversized luxury SUV whose disparaging
nickname *is* the point. It is the rolling argument for his self-regard, and the
thing he parks inches from a homeless man's tent without a second thought. Treat
it as a character: **the same vehicle in every appearance.**

## Visual (house style: hyper-realistic documentary photography)

- A very large, glossy black BMW SUV badged X8 — slab sides, huge blacked-out
  kidney grille, dark wheels, privacy glass. Immaculate, recently valeted, beading
  water. It dwarfs everything near it; in the car park it straddles two bays
  beside the sagging tent — the size contrast is the class map.
- **Warmth as argument:** this is one of the few places warmth is allowed — the
  headlights and DRLs bleed gold through Welsh fog on the night drive (beat 4
  approach), the only heat in an otherwise cool-neutral frame. Opulence and a
  faint menace.
- Shot observationally: from a high angle over wet tarmac, or low past the tent,
  not as a glamour car advert. The gloss should read as obscene next to the tent,
  not aspirational.
