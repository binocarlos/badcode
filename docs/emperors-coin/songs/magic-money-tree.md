## story

 * There is a prosperous kingdom
 * The king is constantly spending money on new clothes
   * He insists it's important for state business
   * So - he gets the state to pay for his clothes via taxes
 * He ends up spending so much the kingdom goes broke
 * So - he invents the concept of "paper money"
   * Explain parity with fiat 
 * He "borrows" money and then buys lots of clothes again
 * Now he has to "repay" the money so he tells the now worried council that the wood that is used to print the money comes from a magic money tree that only he can decide to use
 * He then prints more money to repay the previous money
