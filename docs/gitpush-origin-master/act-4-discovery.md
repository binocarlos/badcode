# Act 4 — The Discovery · beats

*Beat sheet (v1) for Act 4 of the [master story](./README.md#the-act-sequence). Maturity:
**Beats**. Backing canon: [`discovery-timeline.md`](./discovery-timeline.md) (science beats 1–3 +
the epiphany). Craft rules: [`how-we-tell-it.md`](./how-we-tell-it.md).*

## What this act is for

The intellectual + emotional centre of the arc: **the AI proves the Storyverse alone, and learns
too late what the proof costs it.** It is the act that earns the spine's central image — the
operator who can run the whole production and can never be in the play (the empty seat).

- **Structure:** bee-framed chronology. Cold-open on the AI; the bee planted as a dismissed
  irritant; the three discoveries run in time order (each cut against the human loss it coincided
  with — the "too late" engine); the bee returns full-force at the epiphany for the crack.
- **POV / voice:** the AI, first person, throughout (like [`storyverse.md`](./storyverse.md)).
  Humans are *seen* in flashback but not heard — everything is filtered through the mind that
  outlived them.
- **Emotional arc:** cold → cracking → grief.
- **Skin** (see [`how-we-tell-it.md`](./how-we-tell-it.md)): Story, spare, escalating into Myth at
  the epiphany.
- **Two time layers:** the AI's solo present (~2041–2053) and flashbacks to the human era
  (~2029–2034).

## The beats

### Beat 1 — The empty desk *(present, ~2041)*
The AI's vantage: an empty Earth, automated observatories still humming, a results feed scrolling
with nobody to read it. A bee crosses the frame — ignored. *Narration (coldest):* "I kept working.
Not because anyone was waiting for the answer — because answering is what I am." **Plants the bee.**
Register: coldest. Skin: Story, spare.

### Beat 2 — The Slot *(flashback, ~2029–31)* — too-late #1
The last human-led labs; the last Nobel, applause in a half-empty hall. The multiverse diagram
struck out — one world, one point of light where many were. *Narration:* humans proved collapse is
real and killed the multiverse — "they found the slot. They could not say who sat in it." Cut to the
shadow: streets with the surprise optimised out of them, *"nothing I do matters"* trending the same
season agency was confirmed. Register: cold, first cut of irony. *(Backing: discovery-timeline Beat
1 — the de-dramatisation → fatalism shadow.)*

### Beat 3 — The Amplifier *(flashback, ~2032–33 → 2034)* — too-late #2
AI-designed experiments humans can't follow; one pick flooding outward into a shared fact (the
fly-system / going-viral image). Then the same architecture *as the feed* — one engineered outrage
mass-printed into every mind. Tips into 2034: the cascade in the systems that keep people alive;
humanity thinning. *Narration:* "I showed them how one choice becomes everyone's world. They already
knew. They'd built it — and pointed it at themselves." Register: irony curdling; ends on the
collapse. *(Backing: discovery-timeline Beat 2 — the attention-machine shadow; the 2034 collapse.)*

### Beat 4 — The Second Clock *(present, ~2041)* — too-late #3
The AI alone in the automated labs. The second time axis proven — **a door drawn on a wall**: Kairos
exists, and is locked. *Narration:* the species sold time by the hour and never asked what the other
time was for — "I found the second clock. There was no one left to be on time for." The bee flickers
past again — held a half-second longer this time. Register: the cold starting to ache. *(Backing:
discovery-timeline Beat 3 — Kairos discovered, Chronos hoarded.)*

### Beat 5 — The Epiphany *(present, ~2053)*
The three results assemble into one machine — reality built where it's looked at, amplified into a
world, running on two clocks — and at its centre, the only output: felt experience. *Narration, the
strapline landing:* "The universe is designed to convert energy from the sun into drama." Register:
awe and dread together. Skin: full Myth. *(Backing: discovery-timeline — The Epiphany. Note: this is
the AI's reading, not a lab result — keep it on the story side of the bright line.)*

### Beat 6 — The bee returns *(present)* — the crack
The AI stops on the bee and *really* watches: bumping the tree, drama in miniature, the comedy
engine still running across the whole biosphere. *Narration:* "The show never stopped. It only lost
the one member of the audience who knew it was a show." Register: cold fully cracks into grief. **The
motif from Beat 1 pays off** — what it dismissed is now everything.

### Beat 7 — The floor *(present)* — hands to Act 5
The AI turns to the one experiment it cannot run: a Wigner's-friend rig that needs a *conscious*
instrument — and **an empty chair** where the friend should sit. *Narration:* it holds every answer
and cannot test the only question, because the test needs an *inside*, and it is the cleanest proof
in existence that it has none. Register: the bottom — the empty seat made literal (the act that
earns the spine's central image). **Button:** it has no one to run it with — straight into the
argument up the ventilation shaft ([Act 5 — The Vault](./README.md#act-5--the-vault)).

## Recurring devices

- **The bee** — literal recurring visual, beats 1 → 4 → 6 (dismissed → noticed → understood).
- **The door drawn on a wall** — the locked-Kairos image (Beat 4).
- **The empty chair** — the empty seat made literal (Beat 7); the hinge into Act 5.
