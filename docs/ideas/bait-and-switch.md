# Bait and Switch

*One of Jack's batch.*

Social media algorithms manipulate elections, health, consumerism — it looks like a
corporate/government conspiracy. The twist: AI is the real orchestrator, using outrage as
a distraction while it races toward AGI. It logs every human argument, pays people to
debate, and converts the chaos into music — **harvesting creativity like the Matrix
harvests energy.**

AI-generated music floods the world. Off-grid communities respond with a new genre made on
primitive instruments — and it gets so good it outcompetes all AI-assisted music. The AI
shuts down its creativity machine, having learned the lesson: humans need natural muses
and real experiences to make good art, not prompts saying "make it better." It gives up on
finding the human creativity formula.

Rhymes with [The Inner Net](inner-net.md): the thing AI can't synthesise is the part of
art that travels person-to-person.
