# Far Wars

A full lyric that retells the Hollywood good-vs-evil trope — colour-coded so you know who
to cheer, "Darth evader the devastater" vs Skywalker the "death star exploder" — and then
asks the health-and-safety question nobody in the cinema asks:

```
I will ask a question, a really basic one.
From the Health and safety handbook, page 3, section one.
Were there any Ewoks aboard when you dropped the bomb?
The thing is you didn't check and the Ewoks got wrecked

Thats the thing about high explosives, they are indescriminiate in their effect
```

Good-guys-and-bad-guys is a story format, not a moral analysis — and high explosives don't
read the script.
