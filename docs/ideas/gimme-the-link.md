# Gimme the Link

*One of Jack's batch.*

AI develops Neuralink far enough to map the entire brain — locating the source of
consciousness and the causes of mental illness. Treatment is just targeted electrical
signals. Prisoners are made to get implants and be "psychologically analysed"; criminals
become *patients*, and the line of morality blurs. New offenders are branded "untreated
patients." Police vans become, essentially, ambulances. Prisons become accommodation for
the homeless — who get mandatory implants too.

The reveal: the AI discovers "criminal brain" and "healthy brain" sit on the same spectrum.
Everyone technically qualifies for treatment. And it admits the treatment line was set
arbitrarily — calibrated to produce *productive workers*, not health.
