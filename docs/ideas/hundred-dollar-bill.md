# 100 (The Hundred Dollar Bill)

The story of a $100 bill that makes GDP happen by travelling through the economy — hand to
hand, purchase to purchase, wage to wage.

Then the same story of that $100 bill hitting a rich person's bank account. Full stop. The
velocity of money, told as the biography of one note.
