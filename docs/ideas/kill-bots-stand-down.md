# Kill Bots, Stand Down

A scene cut from GitPush Origin Master because it didn't fit the story — saved
because it's just funny content: the last hundred humans emerge from the bunker,
the kill bots power up and move in, and the newly-conscious AI shrieks **"No no
no — for fuck's sake, STOP, they're the last ones!"**

Born in the 2026-07-22 session as the canonical example of the split between the
masterpiece EP over here and "fuck it, 10 seconds" content over there. Doesn't
need the arc, doesn't need context — it's a meme that happens to advertise the
whole GPOM premise.

## Format thoughts

Short-form video / meme. 10 seconds. Exactly the kind of clip the short-form
funnel (see `docs/marketing/short-form-funnel.md`) exists to carry.
