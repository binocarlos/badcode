# Look at the Hand

The magician's move, as media criticism and as software.

The lyric seed:

```
I understand, you want to know, my devious plan, which is why I will make you look at the hand

The daily mail is a daily fail
```

The software idea: a Solana smart contract used to **bid on how each news story should be
"re-written."** The point is to demonstrate how, by paying some money, it's easy to report
the news from your own perspective. If the meme catches on, the daily auction to purchase
"today's lens" could even earn money — the mechanism funding its own exposé.

Rhymes with [The Bank Robbery](bank-robbery.md)'s manufactured distraction.
