# Management Failure

A two-word recurring catchphrase format. Down the pub: England lost the football
again — *"management failure."* All those times at work you feel like shit? Not
your fault — *"management failure."* Same energy as football fans who adore the
manager until one mistake flips the whole crowd (the Too Short riff: we love him,
he's German, doesn't matter — one bad day and *"you're a fucking prick, Tony"*).

The insight under it, said straight in session: **"we love the drama — that's
our voice."** The catchphrase is a tiny vessel for the big BadCode point:
systemic failure gets blamed downward; the fault sits with management — of the
team, of the company, of the country, of the species.

## Format thoughts

Micro short-form — ten-second clips, endlessly repeatable, one scenario per
clip, always landing on the same two words. Meme-tier for the funnel.
