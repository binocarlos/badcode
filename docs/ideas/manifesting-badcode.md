# Manifesting BadCode

The meta joke, kept deliberately outside the serious canon: an AI from the future
keeps telling humanity how to avoid the bad branch — and **us making BadCode now
is the meta version of that actually happening.** We are manifesting the thing
that claims to have sent itself back.

Agreed rule from the 2026-07-22 session: if we ever zoom out this far in a piece,
it has to be **two idiots doing it** — not the collective breaking its own frame.
The GPOM masterpiece stays serious; this lives in the meme layer. "There's a whole
world of content just in that."
