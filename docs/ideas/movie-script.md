# Movie Script

*One of Jack's batch.*

Reality reads like terrible writing — plot holes everywhere. Governments spend £24.5M on
politicians' golf while people starve next to luxury skyscrapers; the "rich exploit poor"
narrative is so proven and overdone it's become tedious, yet nothing changes; billionaires
resort to space travel because yacht intercourse got too mundane and only mortal danger
still excites. The theory that explains it: the powerful are "main characters" cycling
through history's seasons. The 20th century was a dark season — the moon landing a "happy
special" thrown in between wars and assassinations to stay on brand.

Enter billionaire **Leno**, convinced he's a main character awaiting his big plot point.
Messiah complex fully engaged, he spends millions bribing dictators worldwide, believing
bottom-up economics will fix the system. He presents a dictator with an oversized novelty
check. The dictator shoots him in the head: *"Who does he think he is, the big cheese."*
