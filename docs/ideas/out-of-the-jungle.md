# Out of the Jungle

Every human conflict, underneath, has been competition for scarce resources — our
economics, our politics, our evolutionary psychology all assume scarcity. We're now at the
threshold where fusion, robotics, and AI could create genuine abundance, if our thinking
can evolve to match our technology. The lyrics exist in full:

```
Scarcity, fear and violence.
Humans emerge from the silence.
Forged in fires with not enough.
Burning hunger, life is tough.

No Tesco or netflix or chill
Everything that moves will kill
Moving around to different places
This is survival on a daily basis

What would happen if there was enough
An abundant resource of stuff
Would we still be fighting each other
Or has our enemy become our brother?

Nuclear fusion, robots, AI
This combination has arrived
A magic box of tricks and solutions
This is the next step of evolution
```

The story: two tribes live in peace until drought turns friends into enemies — and the
enmity outlives the drought. A mysterious metal box with a single button appears on the
ridge between them; neither side dares touch it. In the next famine a lost girl, Kira,
presses it and it produces food without end. The tribes nearly go to war *over the
abundance machine itself* until children press the button together and food rolls down
both sides of the ridge. An elder laughs: "We're about to fight over abundance itself!
Have we lost our minds?"

Frame story: Professor Chen's "Post-Scarcity Economics 101," present day. "With renewable
energy, we have infinite power from the sun. With robotics and AI, infinite labor. With
asteroid mining, infinite raw materials. **The only scarcity left is artificial** — created
by outdated economic systems designed for a world that no longer exists." Her famous
closing question: *"So I ask you — when will we press the button?"*

A heavily expanded production package exists: the tribes become the Chacoben and Apiacer
in a fictional Amazonia, with Luis the exiled agronomist, Isabella the girl, and the button
recast as a meteorite chest of alien tools.
