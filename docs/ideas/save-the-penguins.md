# Save the… Penguins

*One of Jack's batch.*

In the UK, people donate around £1.7 billion more to animal charities than to
human-related causes. The AI is perplexed at our need to save cute things and project
guilt. The most efficient way to balance an ecosystem is to leave it alone and let
evolution do its thing; the actual problem — humanity's effect on the planet — gets shown
to us by Attenborough once a year and forgotten the rest. It should be run by biologists
who understand what's necessary; instead, letting *anything* go extinct is a crime to us.

The kicker, in the AI's voice: it would probably let us all die and keep a few of us
around to make the documentaries.
