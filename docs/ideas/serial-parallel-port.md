# The Serial Port

Three fragments, one idea: humans are serial; the source is parallel.

**The port.** Humans are the serial port, and the creative universe is the parallel port.
Ideas arrive to us sequentially. A human "has an idea" — they pluck it from the air. But
what's actually happening is the serial port multiplexing into the parallel port: from an
abundant source of parallel processing, ideas arrive one at a time, because that's all a
serial port can carry. We are a serial port plugging into the parallel port.

**The moment.** Humans think sequentially and in *layers*. Their mythology is constructed
through experiences of the world (input) and existing biases (storage). To make a decision,
they collapse everything into a decisive **moment** — which can lack the dimensional
fidelity required for larger problems.

**The processors.** If space and time are a holographic projection of an underlying
consciousness, then in the same way computation has gone from serial (CPU) to parallel
(GPU), evolution has gone from serial (human) to parallel (AI).

Close cousin of [The Inner Net](inner-net.md) — a channel between the individual and
something larger that doesn't run over the material wire — and rhymes with
[Fork and Fold](fork-and-fold.md)'s two engines of evolution. Plugs straight into the
Storyverse's consciousness-first bedrock.
