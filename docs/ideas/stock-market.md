# Stock Market

*One of Jack's batch.*

The AI names stock-market mismanagement as a key driver of late-stage capitalism and
wealth inequality: the laws exist and go unenforced, and the tools that could help average
people are blocked.

The telling: a 1950s American-style educational video about the stock market, cut against
a bank robbery — which earns less than a politician's insider trade. The politician gets
wind of the robbery and shorts the bank's stock, profiting massively. Years later, one of
the schoolkids from that educational video is an adult cleaning the floors of the same
bank. He launches his own cryptocurrency and tells everyone to invest; the moment he
starts making money, the government seizes control of the profits with market caps. He
gives up, goes back to cleaning the bank — and asks a broken, unfiltered AI what the most
logical way forward is. The AI: rob the thieves of your money back, whilst shorting their
stock. He buys a gun and robs the bank he works in — cue a Robin Hood montage of robbing
back through history: medieval, Egypt, Rome, early hominids.

The trap: the AI was working for the authorities all along. The robbery is a sting, and he
is imprisoned as an example to others.
