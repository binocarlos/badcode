# Storyverse, Standalone

The Storyverse — the physics the AI works out in GitPush Origin Master
(participatory collapse, "it's not in two places at once, it's not decided yet",
hierarchical entanglement, multidimensional time) — could stand **on its own as
art**, separate from the GPOM telling. Its own website, its own concept: a
fantasy-sci-fi account of the nature of the universe, the stage the Greeks and
Plato kept pointing at, on which humanity is supposed to create drama.

From the 2026-07-22 session: it's the Arthur-Morgan-washed-up-on-a-beach side
quest of GPOM — and "the storyverse in its own right is art that stands separate
from the rest of the bigger concept." Part love letter, part fuck-you to the
Rick-and-Morty-multiverse crowd: the multiverse is one interpretation; this is
the other one, and it says *you* matter because there is no infinite copy of you.

The register to keep: a named theory with structure that, if you squint, could
stand up in a potential universe. Based-on, not lectured — don't over-indulge
the physics.

Canon source of truth stays
[`stories/gitpush-origin-master/storyverse.md`](../stories/gitpush-origin-master/storyverse.md);
this idea is about giving it its own front door.

Research pointer (via Conor, top of Kai's bookmarks): **"The Landscape of
Consciousness"** — the survey mapping every theory of consciousness by category.
The reference map for keeping the Storyverse's consciousness-science grounded-ish.
