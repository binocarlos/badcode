# Karen Will Lead the Revolution

> **Source of truth** for the Karen story. The comic and the song derive from
> this folder. See [`../storytelling.md`](../storytelling.md) for the method and
> [`../voice.md`](../voice.md) for tone.

A New York middle manager, laid off by AI, weaponises the one thing she's great at
— complaining — and her "I want to speak to your manager" escalates from a phone
box to the President to the AI itself, and into the revolution history remembers.
