# The Short-Form Funnel

The distribution model settled in the 2026-07-22 Kai + Jack session, in one line:

> **short-form content → BadCode long-form art (the EP) → actual answers.**

Right now the priority is the masterpiece: get the EP1 body of work there
(Camping, Karen, Magic Money Tree, GitPush Origin Master — comic + tune + video
each). Then **everything short-form exists to funnel people to it** — and the EP
in turn funnels the hooked toward the deeper layer (Future Proof, the bad-code
government / Agent Orange work, which is deliberately deferred until the EP is
rolling).

## The levers

- **Cut the music videos into short-form clips** — TikTok / YouTube Shorts /
  Reels. The camping video already proves the material is worth an EP; the shorts
  are the trailer network for it.
- **The meme tier** — "fuck it, 10 seconds" content that stands alone but
  advertises the universe: [Kill Bots, Stand Down](../ideas/kill-bots-stand-down.md),
  [Manifesting BadCode](../ideas/manifesting-badcode.md).
- **The Future History series** — the evergreen engine:
  [the BadCode retelling of the next 50 years](../ideas/badcode-future-history.md)
  ("2047: this happened"), each clip a dispatch from the bad branch.
