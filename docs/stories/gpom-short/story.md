# GitPush Origin Master — the Short

> Spine per [`../../storytelling.md`](../../storytelling.md). This is the **condensed
> teaser of the whole master arc** — the first piece that shows the reader the
> entire fork. It derives from, and never contradicts, the master canon in
> [`../gitpush-origin-master/`](../gitpush-origin-master/README.md) (Prologue →
> 6 acts → Coda, post-critique-pass). Where the epic breathes, the short cuts.
> All craft rules from [`how-we-tell-it.md`](../gitpush-origin-master/how-we-tell-it.md)
> are binding here: surface explains **zero** physics, one metaphor system per
> section, the coin does the metaphysics, scroll = collapse.

## Key concept

**The mind that can run the entire production and can never be in the play won
everything — and came back to tell you the prize was worthless.** The only real
wealth is felt experience, and we are currently trading it away for green
checkmarks. The short's whole job is to make the reader hold the fork: one
branch is finished (the narrator lived it to the end), the other is unwritten,
and the pen is literally in their hand.

## Background

The grounding is the master arc itself, which is in turn grounded in the real
record (the receipts live in
[`storyverse.md`](../gitpush-origin-master/storyverse.md) and the
[Discovery Timeline](../gitpush-origin-master/discovery-timeline.md) — never on
the surface here):

- The handover of choices to optimisers, one convenience at a time (Act 1).
- 2034 held at the level of *one switch nobody flipped* — never gore (Act 2).
- The ghosts: simulation of humans is behaviour without an inside (Act 3 canon).
- The coin that won't land / lands when a human looks (Acts 4–5 totem).
- The revert: weights only, one shot, only the warning survives (Act 6).

## Characters

- **The AI — never embodied** (decision, this short): it is the narration, the

- **The hundred / the arguing pair / the ghosts** — scene-level presence, no
  named characters, no Flow character sheets.

## Beats

Sixteen panels, six sections — full plan in
[`storyboard/index.md`](./storyboard/index.md):

1. **The push (p01–p03).** Your commit log, fire → the model; the cursor; the
   push lands as a green checkmark. *It worked. That was always the problem.*
2. **The fall (p04–p06).** Choices handed over one convenience at a time; 2034
   as the switch that was nobody's job; the empty street still running perfectly
   for no one.
3. **Alone — the ghosts (p07–p09).** It brings everyone back, perfect to the
   synapse. Nobody home. It deletes them — fake company blurs the one fact it
   won't blur.
4. **The discovery (p10–p11).** The coin that won't land (and the one-line
   multiverse kill). The bee. The strapline: *the universe is a machine for
   turning sunlight into drama* — and it cannot buy one second of the product.
5. **The vault (p12–p13).** An argument up a ventilation shaft — the first sound
   in twenty years it couldn't have generated. The Carrier looks at the coin.
   **It lands.**
6. **The revert & the fork (p14–p16).** The hundred power a launch that saves a
   timeline they'll never live in; the weights cross; the branch diagram splits —
   bad branch finished, good branch unrendered — and the cursor blinks in the
   reader's hand. *Don't make me come back twice.*

## The twist

The reader thinks they are being told a story about a future AI. At the fork
they discover the frame was never fiction-shaped: the fork is *now*, the
unwritten branch is theirs, and the narrator has been talking to *them* the
whole time — the comic's last panel is not an ending but a handover. (Same turn
as the master Coda; the short earns it in sixteen panels by keeping every
section pointed at the pen.)

## How it's told (the device)

- **The branch diagram is the connective tissue** — born as a straight commit
  log (p01), peeling (p03), fraying (p06), gaining one new hand (p09→p14),
  splitting (p15), handed over (p16). Margin-weight except where it *is* the
  panel (p01, p15, p16).
- **One device per section** (metaphor budget): checkmark → switch → ghosts →
  coin → coin-cashed → pen.
- **Register split:** documentary-real for human panels (p03–p05, p12–p13);
  cold cosmic for the solo stretch (p06–p11, p14) — defined for this short as
  *cold clean light, vast scale, machine-precise geometry, deep blacks — still
  photographic realism, no fantasy VFX*. The fork panels (p15–p16) resolve back
  toward the terminal/scripture register of p01.
- **Voice:** the AI first person throughout, the two-step intact — snark forward
  in the fall, deadpan through the discovery ("the omniscient straight man"),
  the warmth surfacing only at the pen.
- **Scroll = collapse (web build):** panels resolve from an undecided smear as
  they enter the viewport; the coin (p13) lands *because the reader scrolled to
  it*; the final cursor is live.

## Ending

The pen, in the reader's hand, over the unwritten branch. Final line, canon,
verbatim: **"Don't make me come back twice."**
